Two-Ingredient Dough Garlic Knots

These garlic knots begin with the "internet famous" 2-ingredient dough.
Add a few more ingredients and you'll have incredible garlic knots to serve with an Italian meal.v

Ingredients

Bread Dough

  • 1 cup self-rising flour

  • 1.00 cup whole fat plain Greek yogurt

  • ¼ teaspoon granulated garlic

  • ¼ teaspoon Italian seasoning

  • 1 pinch salt

Garlic Topping

  • 1 tablespoon melted butter

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • ½ teaspoon dried parsley

  • ½ teaspoon garlic granules

Directions:

Step 1

Combine flour, yogurt, garlic granules, Italian seasoning, and salt in a bowl; mix to combine.

Step 2

Dust work surface with more flour. Transfer dough to work surface. Knead dough for 8 to 10 minutes with the palm of your hand.
Add more flour (1 teaspoon at a time) as needed to keep dough from being too sticky.

Step 3

Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat (such as Silpat®).

Step 4

Shape dough into a rounded loaf. Using a bench scraper, cut off 8 wedges of dough.
Roll each wedge of dough into a rope shape and tie into knots.
Place knots onto the prepared baking sheet.

Step5

Bake in the preheated oven until knots are lightly browned, 10 to 12 minutes.

Step 6

Meanwhile, combine melted butter, oil, garlic granules, and parsley for topping in a small glass bowl.
Brush mixture over the warm knots.

Recipe Tip

You can make self-rising flour by adding 1/4 teaspoon salt
and 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder to all-purpose flour to equal 1 cup.
